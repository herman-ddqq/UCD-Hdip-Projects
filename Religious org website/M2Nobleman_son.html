Healing of the Nobleman’s Son

Detailed Description

The account of the Healing of the Nobleman’s Son is found in John 4:46-54. A nobleman approached Jesus in Capernaum, imploring Him to heal his son who was near death. Jesus assured him that his son would live. The nobleman believed Jesus' words and returned home, discovering that his son was healed at the very hour Jesus had spoken.

This miracle demonstrates the power of faith and the authority of Jesus' word, transcending distance and circumstance.
