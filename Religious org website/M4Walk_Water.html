Jesus Walking on Water

Detailed Description

The account of Jesus walking on water is found in Matthew 14:22-33. After feeding the 5000, Jesus sent His disciples ahead of Him by boat while He went to pray alone. During the night, a strong wind arose, and the disciples were terrified when they saw Jesus walking towards them on the water.

Jesus reassured them, saying, "Take courage! It is I. Don’t be afraid." Peter asked to come to Jesus on the water, and he walked on the water towards Jesus but began to sink when he noticed the wind. Jesus immediately reached out His hand and caught him.

This miracle illustrates Jesus' divine authority over nature and serves as a lesson about faith.
