Feeding of the 5000

Detailed Description

The miracle of feeding the 5000 is recorded in John 6:1-14. A large crowd followed Jesus to the Sea of Galilee, and He was moved with compassion for them as they were like sheep without a shepherd.

As evening approached, Jesus asked His disciples how they could feed the multitude. A boy with five barley loaves and two fish offered his lunch, and Jesus took the loaves, gave thanks, and distributed them to the people.

Miraculously, everyone ate and was satisfied, and there were twelve baskets of leftovers collected afterwards, demonstrating Jesus' provision and generosity.
