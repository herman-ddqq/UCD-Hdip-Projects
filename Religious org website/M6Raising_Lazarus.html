Raising Lazarus

Detailed Description

The raising of Lazarus is one of Jesus' most profound miracles, found in John 11:1-44. Lazarus, a close friend of Jesus, fell ill and died before Jesus arrived.

Upon reaching Bethany, Jesus was met with mourning and despair. Despite Lazarus being in the tomb for four days, Jesus declared that he would rise again.

With a simple command, "Lazarus, come forth," Jesus demonstrated His power over death, restoring Lazarus to life and affirming His identity as the Resurrection and the Life.
