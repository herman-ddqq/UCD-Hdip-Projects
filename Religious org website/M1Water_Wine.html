Turning Water into Wine

Detailed Description

The account of Jesus turning water into wine is found in John 2:1-11. This miracle took place at a wedding in Cana of Galilee, where the hosts ran out of wine. Jesus' mother, Mary, informed Him of the situation. He instructed the servants to fill six stone jars with water. When they drew from the jars, the water had miraculously turned into wine, showcasing Jesus' divine power and compassion.

This first miracle of Jesus signifies the joy and abundance He brings, illustrating His glory and prompting His disciples to believe in Him.
