Healing the Blind

Detailed Description

The account of Jesus healing a blind man is found in John 9:1-12. As Jesus was walking, He saw a man who had been blind from birth. The disciples asked Him whether the man’s blindness was due to his sin or his parents' sin. Jesus replied that it was neither, but that the works of God might be displayed in him.

Jesus then made mud with His saliva and applied it to the man's eyes, telling him to wash in the Pool of Siloam. The man went and washed, and he came back seeing, showcasing Jesus' miraculous power and His compassion for the suffering.
